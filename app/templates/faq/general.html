1. How do I get more information about the DMC?

2. How do I become the admin for my organization?

    The instructions for becoming a company administrator can be found on our DMC Wiki FAQs page


3. How do I log in?

    Instructions to login in can be found on our DMC Wiki FAQs page.


4. When creating my account, I did not see my organization in the modal. How do I move forward?

    If you did not see your organization in the drop down menu, please visit DMC Wiki FAQs for more information about why you may be experiencing this issue and some possible solutions.
      If you did not find a solution to your issue by following the link above, please reachout to the DMDII membership team or e-mail us at e-mail and we will get back to you within two business days.


5. How do I visit my organization's profile page?

    To visit your organiaztion profile page, click on My Organization on the top of the page in the menu.


6. How do I view other company profiles?

    You can learn how to find other organization profile pages at the DMC Wiki FAQs.


7. How do I get access to my organization's digital DMC assets?

    Once you have created your account and have chosen your affiliated organization, your name will appear in a queue that the organization admin can see. It is the administrator's role to add users who should have access to your organization's digital assets such as the company profile page, member projects and services. If you are not added to the organization in a resonable ammount of time, try reaching out to your organization adminitrator via e-mail, requesting to be added to the to the list of accepted users.

    All services and assets your organization has access to will then become available to you as well.


8. What is a service and what is the process for running one?

    A service is an application that you can run on the DMC. The user is shown a simple inface with inputs and outputs only. These applications do not require you to install anything on your computer as the applications run in the browser.

    You can begin running a service following the steps outlined on the DMC Wiki Page.


9. What browsers are currently supported?

    You can view our supported browsers on our wiki page.


Member Portal

1. My organization is a member of DMDII, why don't I see the member portal?

    Your organization administrator may not have accepted your request for company membership just yet. You may send a new request to the company administrator to verify you by clicking resend notification.
    You can find this by clicking on your name in the top right hand corner of the screen and choosing My Account.

    The process for getting verified is as follows:
    Your company administrator will send you a token (a secret key) paste this key in the verification token field.

    Follow these steps to submit your verification token:
    1. Click on your name in the top right hand portion of the page.
      1. In the drop-down menu, click on My Account.
    2. Paste in your verification token in the appropriate field.
    3. Click on the green verify button.


2. How does my organization become a member of DMDII?

    You can learn more about DMDII, membership, and it's benefits by following this link to the DMDII website.


3. What useful information can be found in the member portal?

    The member portal contains frequently updated information about recent member news, DMDII events news, a list of all current DMDII members and what projects they are working on. Additionally, you can find a list of all current projects, financial and updated information regarding projects. DMDII will also share quick links such as documents and links to they wish to share with all members.

    Here, you can learn more about getting started with the member portal.


4. Member Portal use cases

5. Administering a Member Organization Profile

    2. Create a company description

Contributing

1. How do I contribute to the DMC?

    You can look at our latest updated code on our Bitbucket repository. If you are interested in contributing code to the DMC, please get in contact with us via e-mail or Slack. You can find all of our modes of contact and more information on our Contact-Us page. You can also help us by building applications that can live in the DMC by building DOME models.


2. I'm not a programer. Can I still contribute or get involved?

    Yes, of course! We are looking for help from anyone who is willing to donate their time. Visit our wiki to find out about other ways of contributing.


Running your own Instance of the DMC in your organization

1. What does hosting the DMC internally in my organization do for me?

    Running an instance of the DMC allows you to host the platform behiond your firewall. This means your organization is responcible for keeping your information protected, but does not need to worry about uploading proprietary information to the internet. The DMC is already a safe place to upload your data, but if you would feel safer with your information being hosted locally, this will be an option.


2. What is the process for this?

    We will offer documentation on the process of setting up a DMC instance to run within your organization in the futute.


3. Can the we get assistance in setting this up in our organization?

    We are still heavily working on development but we will eventually be offering this as a service.
