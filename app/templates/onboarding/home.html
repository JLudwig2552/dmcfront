What is the DMDII Portal?

The portal serves as a one-stop communication and collaboration platform within the Digital Manufacturing Commons for DMDII members. It features tools to stay up to date on the latest news from the Institute, shares project updates, and allows collaboration with other members.

What can I find here?

On the portal you’ll find news, discussions, information about DMDII members, and details about the Institute’s projects.

The portal lists DMDII updates, milestones, and events in a single location so you never miss an announcement from the Institute or other members. A member directory hosts profiles of participating organizations—including their skills and interests—to help facilitate networking and project teaming. On the portal’s project page, you’ll find information about ongoing DMDII projects, from news and updates to timelines and events, to help keep you informed about the wide scope of projects underway at DMDII.

How do I use it?

Begin by contacting your organization’s administrator for your credentials to access the portal. Once you’ve logged in, you’ll find an easy-to-use, customizable interface. Start by creating your profile to tell other members about you and your organization—who you are, what you do, and the skills you bring to DMDII. After your profile is up to date, engage with the DMDII community by following other members and participating in discussions within the forum. Update your projects on the “My Projects” tab, upload documents, and invite other members to engage with you in your work. Check back often for the latest news, discussions, and project updates posted to the portal.

Organizational Steps

  • appoint company admin
  • communicate selection to UI LABS
  • company admin create DMC user account
  • user account is elevated to company admin

Step 1

UI LABS will contact DMDII members via email to begin the on boarding process by selecting a company administrator.
The company administrator will manage the organization's presence on the Portal including profile and user access.

Step 2

Once the administrator is selected for the company, this must be communicated to UI LABS via the email invitation.

Step 3

The designated admin will create a user account on the DMC.

Step 4

UI LABS will confirm the admin, assigning the “Company Administrator Role” to this person for the organization.

Organizational Member Steps

  • create DMC account
  • your organization admin will validate you
  • user self-elevates to organization member
  • enjoy the Portal

Step 1

When users begin creating a DMC account, the first step will be to select the organization. A notification will be sent to your company’s administrator who will then validate your access.

Step 2

The company administrator will grant access to the user, sending the user a “validation token” via email as confirmation.

Step 3

The user will add this “validation token” to their account which will validate their membership.

Step 4

The user will now have access to the company assets on the Portal.

Further Aid

In the unlikely event that your on boarding process is not working properly or should you have further questions or concerns, please consult the FAQ section footer of every page or contact us directly.

Users will NOT be able to access company assets until the organizational steps listed above are completed. It is the responsibility of the organization's admin to grant a individual access to the company assets. If the only menu item visible to you is "About Portal", this signifies that you have not yet been validated as a organizational member. Please contact your organization admin.
